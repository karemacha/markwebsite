Who is Dr. Mark Kaahwa ?

Dr. Mark Kaahwa was born and raised in a humble family in Nyarubingo Village Kyenjojo district, Uganda. Although he almost failed to attend school due to fees challenges, the hand of God has raised him from Primary to doctoral studies with sponsorship throughout. He holds a Bachelor of Social & Philosophical studies, Post graduate Diploma in Education, Masters in Educational Planning & Management and a PhD in Educational Sciences. His areas of expertise include; Educational Technology, Blended Learning, e-pedagogy, electronic media, educational policy, community education, educational management, and leadership.

He is a professional teacher who has not only written on paper and chalkboard but has “written” on the lives of tens to thousands of students. Mark is an accomplished teacher who has taught at all levels of education right from Kindergarten to University. He is currently a Senior Lecturer and Coordinator of Open Distance and Electronic Learning (ODeL) and also in-charge of Community Engagement in the Faculty of Education. He previously served MMU as Ag. Assistant Registrar, a position he held up to 2010 before being transferred to the Faculty of Education, first as assistant lecturer and later in 2011 as Head of Department Pre-service and In-service departments and Ag. Dean from 2013 to 2015.

He has done a lot of community service in the Albertine region in Uganda including but not limited to training Primary School head-teachers in Primary School improvement planning and effective management of schools, training primary school teachers in pedagogical skills and other continuous professional trainings. He has published articles in international peer reviewed journals. He has attended and presented papers at four international conferences in Spain Valencia, Tanzania Mzumbe University, South Africa Cape Town and Austria Vienna. He has successfully supervised undergraduate and postgraduate students. He is also an external examiner in both private and public Universities in Uganda.
