PROJECTS ENGAGED IN

• Investigating the potential of Radio technology as a channel of communication in enhancing small holder banana farmers’ knowledge regarding proper banana farming practices in the Rwenzori Region. Principal Investigator.

• Assessing the role of audio media technology in equipping small holder banana farmers with financial literacy skills in the Rwenzori Region. Principal Investigator.

• Assessment of societal norms, values and attitudes that promote or fight corruption in the Rwenzori region. Assistant Team leader.

• Carrying out needs analysis on the community needs for the people around Kijwiga study Center for Mountains of the Moon University in Uganda. Senior research Assistant.

• Carrying out needs analysis on leadership training program for primary Schools in Kabarole District. Project Leader and Principal Investigator.

• Assessing the post-covid effects on the boy and girl-child education in primary schools in the Rwenzori region. Co. Principal Investigator.

• Eco-home project as a Small Great Project within the framework of Global Minds. The overall goal of the project is to promote green development and tourism economy from the household level among communities in the Rwenzori region, Western Uganda. Co. Principal Investigator.

Am working on; Decentralized Blended E-Learning Solution for Remote Education of In-service Teachers in Resource Constrained Communities of Rwenzori Region, Western Uganda.
